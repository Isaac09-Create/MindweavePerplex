About Microsoft Mindweave

Mindweave is a new Microsoft division focused on AI-powered cognitive enhancement and digital well-being. Our mission: empower your mind with intelligent AI, transforming how you learn, work, and thrive.

  • For Professionals: Boost productivity, decision-making, and information synthesis.
  • For Students & Academics: Personalized learning, research, and academic writing support.
  • For Everyone: Cognitive fitness, mindfulness, and digital well-being tools.

Mindweave integrates seamlessly with Microsoft 365, Azure, and our trusted ecosystem to help you unlock your full potential.
